The Dark Side of Gliamoor

The dark side of Gliamoor was almost uninhabitable and is vastly less populated compared to the light side. Until amulets that would protect you from the extreme conditions were common place very few people even dared to traverse there. The political structure is that of a dictatorship where there is one ruler who rules with an iron fist. He controls all of the means of production for all produce and livestock. This side of the planet is much more varied in the terrain as there are hills, valleys, rivers, mountains, and lakes covering major swaths of the land.

Many of the buildings are new or built within the last hundred years. Many new business have popped as more and more people move to live on the dark side of the planet. With so many new people also spell more chaos and disaster if the influx wasn't handled properly. Sufficed to say structure and orginization are almost non existent here.

The political scene

Malzareth Korr is the first person to venture into the dark side and return alive. This has been his story and help bolster his reputation that let him gain the opportunity to organize a governing body and place himself at the top in the place of power. All business is either in control of the goverment or you are considered a contractor of the goverment and allowed to operate independantly because they basically bribe the government to leave them alone. Malzareth also provides significant "contributions" to key corrupt galactic officals so they turn a blind eye at his less than legal business dealings. He is a fan of labor camps where if someone who lives under his rule and they aren't able to pay his taxes then he sends them to a labor camp to provide free labor harvesting and working the fields of the goverment.

The Ecology

There are vastly more plants native to this side of the planet scattered throughout the land. The extreme cold prevents many plants that can't handle the intense cold to not be reasonable to try and grow here. Strangely enough many of the plants that exist on the light side share a similar visual similarity to many of the plants found on this side. Almost as if as one point this planet didn't always have one side always facing the star it orbits. But what could be powerful enought to prevent a whole planet from spinning on its axis?

The biggest animal of note on this side is the Vaelgrim. It is a massive 10 foot tall dog-like beast that roams the darkness in packs on the hunt for anything to satisfy their omnivore diet. The Vaelgrim are quite inteligent and have strong sense of danger making them naturally warry about people. Malzareth and some of his followers have managed to tame a few of them and sell their fur to be made into all sorts of things as it is extremely soft and quite strong and flexible material.

The Black Market

Like with many places there is a thriving crime ring and a huge black market of less than legal good being bought and sold. Malzareth chooses to ignore this whole operation as long as they pay their taxes he lets them be. This in turn has helped make it so that many honest and law abiding folk don't have much choice to try and run their own business because they can't compete in this enviroment unless they pay top dollar for security around their work to ensure that all of what is your remains that way. Malzareth does employ "soliders" and mercanries who are mainly in charge of "keeping" the peace and keeping the indentured servants in line on the farms and ranches.

It is the blantant atmosphere of thrill seekers and thieves that has inspired many of the wealthy to "contract" these lowlifes to hide and protect specific treasures so that their most precious items are secure. The problem is that the prefered hiding spot of many of the rich is to find a secluded part of the undergound tunnel system and put protection around their treasures and let the natural barriers in addition to their own magic to disuade people from trying to get their hands on it.
