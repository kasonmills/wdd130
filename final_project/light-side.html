The Light side of Gliamoor

The Light side of Gliamoor or the side that faces the sun and is mostly covered by vast farmland and pastures mainly owned by the "mob" crime families who represent the whole political structure. The capital city sits at the highest point of the world and the place where the world gets the most light at any given point.

The Political scene

The Virello Family

The Virello family business is the family that holds all the power because they own the most land and they also own this biggest business on the light side. Their main export is all produce that is not native to Gliamoor but will still thrive in the light. everyone in the Virello family is an Air Genasi and uses their natural magical talents to help all their plants thrive.

The Grenthos clan

The Grenthos clan is a smaller group than the Virello family but not by much. Their main export is livestock. Often they are also in charge of raising and slaughtering their own animals. Their best seller is Vorrak'thul legs. All greater treatment is given to those who actually bear the Grenthos name they aren't against adopting outsiders if they like them enough. Since they have to deal so often with the Vorrak'thul if you are an Earth Genasi or a Fire Genasi so are better equiped to handle them because they can get quite violent if they feel threatened.

The Dreknari syndicate

The Dreknari syndicate is vastly smaller family but just as dangerous. The family consists of only Water Genasi and they are responsible for supplying the other two families with all the water and fertlizer that is needed to maintain the immense farming operations they run. The main issue is the Vorrak'thul are meat eaters and they have no interest in anything else. So they also import hundreds on animals from the dark side of the planet to feed them.

The Ecology

There are so many plants that have been brought to Gliamoor that very few of the native plants are even left. There are patches of wild growth and untamed land but they are the exception not the rule. The most commonly seen and farmed plant that is native to Gliamoor is what is locally known as the "Wailing wheat." This unsuspecting plant is a bright yellow while it grows but turns a dark red when it is ready to be harvested. The unique properties of this plant are that if you wander in a field of it its defense mechanism triggers and the whole field will turn pink and a silent "wail" will begun to be heard in your mind. If you spend too much time wandering in these fields while they remain pink you will be under constant psychic attack as it will deal one d6 psychic damage for every 15 seconds you are within 5 feet of the plant.
