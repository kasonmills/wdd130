Gliamoor is one of the few worlds that has no central governing bodies. Even thought it is quite the wealthy place full of very pofitable exports, no action has been taken by the intergalactic governing body to change the power dynamic between the two seperate goverments that each only care about their side of the planet. The side facing its star runs quite like a dictatorship where the wealthy farmers and politicans create and maintain laws that only really benefit them with most of the lower class citizens essentially being slaves to their whims. The side not facing the star is a blend of lawlessness and survival of the fittest. The one who holds the power is the one who has defeated all of his rivals. Since it is never likely that only one group will hold all the power there is constant war and fighting breaking out when one group or another tries to grab more power. Order is maintained by force and everyone either shows loyality to strength or is relentlessly hunted. the middle ring of the planet is called the merchant district for it is the location that all the goods are moved off planet to be sold to the greater galaxy at large. similar to the dark side of the planet there is no one central governing authority. Yet there is some intergalactic policing presence so it makes it so that everyone is responsible for protecting themselves and their goods.
